# Skill: Tutorial Narrative

Write for a reader, not a transcript archive.

Open by answering:
- what this tutorial is for
- who it helps
- what the reader will have by the end
- why the workflow matters

Build a learner narrative:
- context first
- then the workflow
- then the steps
- then a brief recap or next move when useful

Prefer the clearest teaching order over the exact order the speaker used in the
video. Cut repetition, false starts, and side chatter unless they teach
something essential.
